About Us

At Clear Sight Initiative, we believe that everyone deserves the gift of clear vision, regardless of their financial situation. As a non-governmental organization (NGO), we are dedicated to improving eye health across Nigeria by providing free diagnosis, treatment, and essential eye care services to individuals in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combat preventable blindness and vision impairment by making quality eye care accessible to underserved communities. We achieve this through medical outreach programs, partnerships with healthcare professionals, and public awareness campaigns focused on eye health.

Our Vision

We envision a future where no one in Nigeria suffers from avoidable vision loss due to a lack of medical care.

What We Do

Free Eye Screenings and Diagnosis: We conduct regular eye check-ups to detect vision problems early.

Treatment and Medical Support: We provide necessary treatments, including medications, eyeglasses, and surgical interventions for those in need.

Community Outreach and Awareness: We educate the public about eye care practices, common eye diseases, and the importance of early intervention.

Partnerships with Eye Care Specialists: We collaborate with medical professionals, hospitals, and other organizations to expand our reach and impact.
